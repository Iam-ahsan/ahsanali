Short Bio

Ahsan Ali is a postdoctoral appointee at the Data Science and Learning division of Argonne National Laboratory. He completed his Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at University of Nevada Reno under Dr. Feng Yan. His areas of interests are Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, Big Data, Computer Systems, Performance Optimization.
