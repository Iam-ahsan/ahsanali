Research Experience:

Cooling Cost Optimization of Geographically Distributed Data Centres (Fall 2016)

Advisers: Dr. Oznur Ozkasap
The cooling cost and network delays of geographically distributed data centers are minimized by using inter/intra data center scheduling techniques. To achieve cooling cost optimization and delay minimization, three different workload scheduling techniques are proposed. These techniques are implemented in Java using CloudSim simulator by adding time-varying characteristics to data centers.

Price, Cooling, and Delay Aware Scheduling in the Geographically Distributed Data Centres (2015-2016)

Advisers: Dr. Oznur Ozkasap
The optimization of the data centre cooling cost and SLA violations is proposed. Scheduling is performed based on temperature variation and the user specified request deadlines.

Analysis of Environment-Friendly Energy Efficient Distributed Data Centres (2014-2015)

Advisers: Dr. Oznur Ozkasap
The state of the art scheduling and electricity cost optimization techniques for geographically distributed data centers is analyzed. The techniques are divided into 4 major categories based on their similarities and cost model is proposed.
